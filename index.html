AgroSense 2.0 — Biodegradable Soil Patch
Zero-waste precision agriculture sensor system
Discuss →
Problem

Traditional soil sensors leave hardware waste in fields. Retrieval at scale is costly and impractical for farmers.

The Concept

Biodegradable sensor patches embedded into soil — monitor conditions in real-time, then decompose naturally. Zero retrieval, zero hardware waste.

Why It Matters

Enables dense IoT deployment at agricultural scale without the environmental or logistical cost of hardware recovery.
